HERITAGE & LEGACY

A Journey Through Time, Tradition, and Excellence

FOUNDING PRINCIPLES - 1947

Born in the year of India's independence, Mittal Reserve represents the perfect union of ancient Indian distilling wisdom and European aging techniques. Our founders envisioned a brand that would capture the spirit of a new era—one of opulence, sophistication, and uncompromising quality. Each bottle tells a story of heritage, carrying forward traditions perfected over centuries.

THE ART OF MATURATION

Deep within our stone cellars, beneath the earth where temperature and humidity remain constant, our spirits rest in carefully selected oak barrels. Each cask breathes in the unique climate, absorbing the essence of time itself. Our master distillers monitor every barrel with the patience of artisans, waiting for that perfect moment when age transforms spirit into liquid gold.

BRAND PHILOSOPHY

Mittal Reserve is not merely a drink; it is a time capsule, preserving the spirit of opulence and rarity for the modern collector. We believe that true luxury lies not in abundance, but in scarcity and exclusivity. Each bottle represents hours of craftsmanship, years of aging, and decades of tradition. When you hold a Mittal Reserve, you hold more than whisky—you hold legacy.
